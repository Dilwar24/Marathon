About the Marathon

The Lions Club of Guwahati Elite, in association with IPR Group and Lone Tree Consultants Pvt. Ltd., proudly presents the Marathon for Peace. This distinguished event is scheduled for November 10, 2024, in commemoration of World Science Day for Peace and Development. The marathon promotes the values of peace and tranquility within our urban communities.
